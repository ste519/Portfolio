Overview

Name: Waitless

Format: Web App

Platform: mobile (preferred Pixel 2 or iPhone 6/7/8 Plus)

 

As we previously observed, with limited information, UCSD students struggle to find food efficiently under time restrictions. Therefore, our team decide to create an app for the current UCSD community to enhance their dining experience by making the food selection process easier and more efficient.

 

The web app “Waitless” have two major functionalities: “Wait Time Ranking” and “Favorites List”.

 

Wait Time Ranking: displays wait time ranked from least to the most

First, the user will be shown the current system time. The sorted list of restaurants from the least waiting time to the most will be displayed. This allows our user to access the information within seconds without complicated navigations. Adding on to the current wait time, the user can click on any one of the restaurants to go to the page that shows estimated wait times of specific restaurants. Or, the user can click on the time button to select a preferred time to see the projected wait times, also in sorted order from least to most.

 

Favorites List: stores favorite restaurants

In the current wait time/estimated wait time page, if the user clicks on the little heart button next to the wait time, that specific restaurant will be stored to local storage and be shown on the favorite list page. In addition, the user can “unlike” any restaurant simply by clicking the red heart button on the favorite list page. To remove all liked the restaurant, simply click on the clear button.
